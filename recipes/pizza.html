Pizza

Description

A delicious homemade pizza with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups warm water
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, combine the warm water, yeast, and sugar. Let it sit for 5 minutes, or until the mixture becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes, or until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.
  6. Punch down the dough and divide it into two portions. Roll out each portion into a round pizza crust on a floured surface.
  7. Transfer the pizza crusts to a baking sheet or preheated pizza stone.
  8. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the crusts and top with shredded mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the crust is golden brown.
  10. Remove from the oven, let the pizzas cool for a few minutes, and then slice and serve.
